How To Play
Introduction

Welcome to the Wikirace! This is an real-time, single page desktop game meant to be played individually or with two players. It is inspired by the original Wikipedia games which you can check out here.
Rules

The rules are very simple. When the player starts the game, they are given a starting article and a destination article. The goal of the game is to reach the destination article by clicking through the links on the page in the least amount of time and clicks possible. After the player reaches the destination article, they are given a score based on their performance. If the players are logged in, their score will be saved and available to view on their profile page.
